How to Play KinitoPET

KinitoPET is a game where you are free to begin with any activity you like. Launching a mysterious software you received, the game will bring you to a desktop with a bunch of programmes. Select one of them and begin an intriguing journey of KinitoPET filled with horrible incidents and riveting puzzles.

  • Befriend Kinito. This game is centred around a weird, but lovely creature named Kinito. He is going to become your buddy for the session, but don’t trust him too much, as you never know what is on his mind!
  • Win in every attraction. Apart from the main game, KinitoPET gives you a chance to try out your abilities in Minesweeper and 3D Pinball. Will you beat two of them and figure out more secrets about the universe?
  • Traverse through each file. Search the settings and the files of KinitoPET to reveal certain riddles and hidden pieces. It will take some time to decipher all the codes in the game, so be ready to strain your brains a lot.
  • Obtain sophisticated achievements. KinitoPET is also brimming with diverse accomplishments that can be extremely hard to get. Follow certain patterns and discover the most intriguing facts about the lore of the game.

Is KinitoPET Safe to Play?

Despite the strange features of KinitoPET, such as the ability to affect the files on your own computer, this wonderful game by troy_en is completely safe, doesn’t contain any viruses and available on Steam. Enjoy the unblocked version of the game and unravel the truth about the perilous programme that wants to break free from digital imprisonment.

To bypass the harmful effects of your virtual companion though, you will need to demonstrate a great deal of intelligence. Escape this perilous kingdom in time or you will stay in this horrifying prison forever!
